Food & Beverage









From specialty coffees to pub-style dinners, our food service outlets have the perfect menu for your taste.
SKI Magazine readers consistently rank Wachusett among the Best in the East for On-Mountain Food.
Wachusett is a past winner of  “Best Entrée” award at the annual “Taste of Wachusett” exhibition sponsored by the Wachusett Chamber of Commerce.

Base Lodge







Cafe Food Court
Fuel up for the slopes with a full hot breakfast served every day until 10 a.m. Stop by for lunch or dinner featuring chili, soups, fresh salads, sandwiches, burgers, entrée specials, plus a huge selection of Polar Beverages.








Identity Coffee Lab
Treat yourself to a made-to-order espresso, cappuccino, or latte, paired with delicious baked goods and breakfast sandwiches. Operated by Identity Coffee Lab.










Black Diamond Restaurant
Pub style dining overlooking the slopes serving lunch and dinner daily. Menu features gourmet burgers, pasta, steak tips, specialty entrées and famous NASTAR Nachos. Bar service. MENU








Coppertop Lounge
The perfect après-ski spot with a variety of draft beers including favorites from Wachusett Brewery and other local craft beers. 










The T-Bar
Our wine bar located in the Coppertop Lounge. Enjoy a fine selection of wines & specialty drinks. The T-Bar also features a Tapas menu. Perfect for your après ski fun! VIRTUAL TOUR 








The Core
Looking for a quick treat or beverage? Variety of delicious apple products, cider donuts, fudge, candy & more. Operated by Red Apple Farm.










O’Brien’s Deck Bar & Grill
Now Open – Our newest outlet offering limited selection of draft beers, tasty sandwiches and more.  Located on the deck outside of the base lodge. MENU







On-Mountain Treats







Bullock Lodge Cider House
This charming stone hut was built in the 1930’s by the Civilian Conservation Corp. Operated by Red Apple Farm from Phillipston, MA, Bullock features freshly made hot cider donuts, apple turnovers, fresh fudge, hot cider and coffee.  VIRTUAL TOUR








Waffle Cabin
Enjoy freshly baked Belgian sugar waffles either plain or drizzled with chocolate. A treat you won’t want to miss.







Note: Above Food Service Areas are only open during our Winter Ski Season
