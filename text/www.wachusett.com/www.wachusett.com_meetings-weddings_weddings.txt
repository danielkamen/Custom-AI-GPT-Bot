Weddings











Wachusett Mountain is a wedding venue unlike any other. This is a unique setting with outdoor and indoor venue options. The picturesque background will leave you and your guests in awe, as our excellent staff takes care of every detail. We ensure a day you and your guests will always remember!



We offer a wide variety of wedding themes and locations, from an outdoor country wedding on our spacious green grounds, to a traditional indoor wedding in our expansive base lodge, or even a romantic trip to the summit-we will help create the perfect day for you.
For more information or to see if your date is available, please call 978-464-3175 or email [email protected].
