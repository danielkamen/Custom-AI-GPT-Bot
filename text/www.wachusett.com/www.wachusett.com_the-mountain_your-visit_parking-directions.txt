Parking & Directions









VIP Parking Available
Daily VIP Parking must be purchased in advance online  
VIP Parking guarantees upper lot parking for the Session purchased. Notify the parking attendants when you arrive and they will direct you to a spot.
General Parking is FREE!

There is an $8 change fee for switching reservation dates or sessions.
Directions
ADDRESSES
GPS: 41 Mile Hill Road, Westminster, MA 01473MAIL: 499 Mountain Road, Princeton, MA 01541NO CAR? No problem! Take the Ski Train to Wachusett Station and we’ll pick you up. (select days during ski season, check page for the latest schedule)
Hiker Parking
We Need Your Help! When the Ski Area opens for skiing/riding the main Parking Lot is dedicated for skiers and snowboarders.If you are planning to hike the mountain, please see hiking trail map, and use one of the 7 alternative parking areas:    1.    Wachusett Mountain State Reservation Visitor’s Center    2.    Mountain House Trail    3.    Echo Lake Road (near Mountainside Café)    4.    Echo Lake Trail    5.    West Side Trail    6.    North Road    7.    Bolton Pond Trail (on Bolton Road) If necessary, hikers may also use Parking Lot #2 at the Ski Area.  Thank you for your cooperation.
