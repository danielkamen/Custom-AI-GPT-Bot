Mutual Agreement and Cancellation Policy









Mutual Agreement

To withdraw from the program, return your Student Badge or Season Pass along with your request in writing. For medical issues, we will need a doctor’s note stating the date of injury or illness.
There are No Refunds for bus fees paid to Wachusett Mountain.
 Any and all changes from the current season must be presented to Wachusett no later than April 1 of current ski season
 We will issue you a $20 transferable Wachusett Mountain voucher on sessions not attended. This voucher may only be used during the current season, toward a full price lift ticket and cannot be applied to the following  season. Please Note: THERE ARE NO CASH REFUNDS WITHOUT THE BADGE/PASS INSURANCE.
Badges, Passes, Lessons or Rentals may not be substituted or transferred for any reason. Misuse will result in the loss of privileges. Programs are date and time specific.
There is a $10 clerical fee for changes made after your order is received. Mail or email request for changes or withdrawal before December 24 of current season to Wachusett Mountain Ski Area, Attn: Group Sales, 499 Mountain Road, Princeton, MA 01541 or [email protected]. After December 24 of current season give your request to your program coordinator to hand in the first day of your program.

Cancellation Policies

If your school cancels and we are open: A make-up session will be scheduled. The make-up for that session may not include lessons.
On school cancellation days you can come to Wachusett and receive 50% off lift and rental purchases when you present your Student Badge at Customer Service.
